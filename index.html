Bio

Cyrina Fiallo, born and raised in Miami Florida, grew up wanting to be a contortionist. When she realized she would never quite fit into that small of a box, she decided to study acting.

A short stint in NY working for The Late Show with David Letterman led her to Hollywood, CA. She began studying at Improv Olympic and quickly became part of a popular Teen Improvised Dramedy called "Sunset Heights". Since moving to Los Angeles, she has appeared in over 35 commercials and various television shows including NBC's "Community", CW's "Supernatural", and starred in the MTV film "Made...The Movie". She can currently be seen on Disney's "Good Luck Charlie" playing the role of Vonnie. In her spare time, you might catch her writing a script or singing a song with her band, The Girls with fellow actresses Julianna Guill and Alison Brie.
